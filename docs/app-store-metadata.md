```
Buildout is the all-in-one renovation calculator built for contractors and serious DIYers. Stop guessing on materials. Get accurate estimates in seconds, build professional job quotes, and walk onto every job prepared.

──────────────────────────
7 CALCULATORS IN ONE APP
──────────────────────────

PAINT CALCULATOR
Enter room dimensions or map each wall individually. Adjust for surface texture, number of coats, and deduct doors and windows. Get exact gallons and a broken-down shopping list — full gallons plus quarts.

TILE CALCULATOR
Input room size and tile dimensions. Buildout adds a 10% waste factor automatically and tells you exactly how many tiles and boxes to buy. Never come back from the store for one more box.

GROUT CALCULATOR
Calculate grout by tile size and joint width. Output in pounds and bags so you buy the right amount and waste nothing.

LVP FLOORING CALCULATOR
Room area with a built-in 10% overage for cuts and layout. Optional price-per-box input gives you an instant material cost before you leave the showroom.

CARPET CALCULATOR
Get square footage and square yards — carpet is sold by the yard, and Buildout handles the conversion automatically. Includes 10% waste and optional cost estimate.

STAIRCASE CALCULATOR
Carpet stairs with confidence. Calculate tread and riser coverage separately, with a 15% waste factor built in for the extra cuts stairs demand.

DRYWALL CALCULATOR
Wall area minus doors and windows, converted to 4×8 sheet count. Also estimates joint compound, tape, and screws so one trip to the hardware store covers the whole job.

JOB QUOTING TOOL
──────────────────────────

Build a full job quote in minutes. Add line items with description, quantity, and unit price. Apply a tax rate and Buildout calculates the subtotal, tax, and grand total automatically.
```
